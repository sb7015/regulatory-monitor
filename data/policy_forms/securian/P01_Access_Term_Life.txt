FILED YEAR: 2023
STATUS: Active
APPLICABLE STATES: Texas (TX), California (CA)

DESCRIPTION:
Affordable term life coverage for a set period with level premiums. No cash value. Conversion option to permanent policy. Accelerated and traditional underwriting available. eApp supported.

KEY FEATURES:
- Level premiums for selected term period
- Conversion option to permanent life insurance
- Accelerated underwriting available
- Death benefit paid to beneficiary upon insured's death
- No cash value or surrender charges (term product)
