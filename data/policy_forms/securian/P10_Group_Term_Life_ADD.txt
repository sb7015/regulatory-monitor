FORM NUMBER: 20-12345 and 22-12345
FILED YEAR: 2020 / 2022
STATUS: Active
APPLICABLE STATES: Texas (TX), California (CA)

DESCRIPTION:
Employer-based group term life with integrated AD&D. Basic (1x salary, max $500K) + supplemental (up to 8x, max $2M). Spouse, child, voluntary AD&D options. Age reduction at 70 (65%) and 80 (50%).

AD&D LOSS SCHEDULE:
- Both hands/feet or sight of both eyes: 100%
- One hand and one foot: 100%
- One hand/foot or sight of one eye: 50%
- Thumb and index finger same hand: 25%
- Loss must occur within 200 days of accident
